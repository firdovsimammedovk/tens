Getting Started

Use this page as the quickest entry point into TT-Forge.

Start here

#. Read the :doc:`overview <index>` to understand how TT-Forge fits together.
#. Choose a frontend in :doc:`forge-frontend-compiler/index` based on your source framework.
#. Review the :doc:`forge-backend-compiler/index` to understand how models are lowered to Tenstorrent targets.
